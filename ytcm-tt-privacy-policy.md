**Privacy Policy**

Last updated: March 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how YTCM Research ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our application.

### 1. Data We Process

Our application analyzes publicly available data from TikTok. We do not require users to create an account or log in.

We may process:

- Public TikTok content (such as videos, metadata, and trends)
- Technical data necessary to operate the service (e.g., server requests)

We do not collect personal data directly from users of this application.

### 2. Purpose of Processing

The data is processed solely to:

- Analyze music and cultural trends on TikTok
- Provide aggregated insights and analytics

### 3. Data Storage

We do not store personal data of users.

Public TikTok data may be temporarily cached to improve performance and stability of the service.

### 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer data to third parties.

### 5. Legal Basis (GDPR)

Processing is based on legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR), as the application analyzes publicly available data for research and analytical purposes.

### 6. Data Location

All services are hosted within the European Union.

### 7. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect data and ensure secure operation.

### 8. Your Rights

* Request access to your data
